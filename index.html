HOW TO PLAY
1To win you must reduce the Trolley Master's stack to 0! You and the Trolley Master each get 5 cards from 77 real Aldi products.
2Your card flips face-up. Study your product's nutrition stats and price.
3You can see all 8 battle rules — pick one strategically. e.g. 💪 Highest Protein or 💰 Lowest Price. That rule decides the winner.
4The Trolley Master's card flips. Whoever wins the chosen stat takes both cards.
5Each rule gets a red USED stamp once played — you can't pick it again. Choose carefully!
6After all 8 rules are used, they remix with opposite directions — Highest becomes Lowest and vice versa. First to drain the other's deck wins.
🤝 TIES
If both products score the same, both cards go to the central pile. The winner of the next round takes them all!
🔄 YOUR SWAP POWER
Before picking a rule, you can SWAP your card with the Trolley Master's. Think theirs looks better? Steal it. You get 2 swaps per game — use them wisely.
